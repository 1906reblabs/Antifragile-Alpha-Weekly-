Screening Methodology ✓ Free Preview

We use a Barbell factor model combining value, quality, momentum, and catalysts. Academic evidence for predictive factors guides our screen. All candidate stocks must clear the following criteria:

  • Value: Low P/E or P/B versus peers and history (e.g. SHP P/E 18.8 vs retail peers; Growthpoint P/NAV 0.88). High free-cash-flow yields or dividend yields are a plus. Evidence: the value factor yields persistent returns.
  • Quality: Strong profitability (ROE/ROIC), stable earnings, low leverage. E.g. SBK ROE ~18% with healthy capital. Evidence: quality firms outperform over cycles.
  • Momentum/Technical: Upward price trends, RSI/MA signals, volume surges. We require at least moderate positive 3–6m momentum.
  • Catalysts (required): Each pick needs ≥1 clear catalyst in 3–12m: rate cuts, commodity upcycles, regulatory change, M&A, or earnings turnarounds.
  • Macro Overlay: SARB policy, rand FX, inflation, load-shedding. We favor cyclical longs if rates ease, and gold/miners if inflation stays low.
  • Liquidity: Minimum avg vol thresholds (>0.5m/day). E.g. IOC ~0.87m vs 4Sight ~0.15m.
  • Other Signals: Insider buys/sells, institutional ownership, short interest (qualitative).

Scoring Rubric (0–100): Each stock scored on (Value, Quality, Momentum, Liquidity, Catalysts), weighted to prefer undervalued + high catalyst probability.
